World Photography Day

18 August 2020


Cameras have been with me since i was born ! Yes, my father is a very big fan of capturing memories and because he loved being infront of the camera, i got a chance to use the Camera much more. And i swear, DSLRs are the best invention of humans indeed. They're so amazing ! It like getting HD memories imprinted directly from the brain ! Ideas in the head and boom ! your ideas are visible to all. Its like creativity and ideas that are in our minds can be seen by all and that's so amazing.
I have shot more than 100,000 images on my DSLR over the span of 7 years, and the majority includes sunsets, sunrises, birds, more wildlife, landscapes, parents, cousins and many more humans. Still today you find a different prespective when you hold that marvelous tool today. And it signifies how there are millions of things around us to admire but we dont.Sometimes the most ugliest things look amazing and thats what life is, its just the perspective that makes us differentiate between beautufil and ugly. But the almighty gave us a beautiful brain that could create something that would help us realize that ! And the irony is the same that its that brain that started to differentiate on the first !
