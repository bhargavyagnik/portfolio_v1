COVID Dashboard

June 10, 2020


2020 has been a very transforming period for me because COVID took off and then the world just stopped ! With the no. of cases increasing each day, it felt like dooms day was nearer and I was inquisitive to find out when would this get over. Me being an Data Lover, i thought lets see some patterns or try to understand covid. While my dad (who's a Doctor) was busy trying to understand how to detect the covid and understand its spread, I thought to apply some visualizations and statistics.
So i took an opensource datasheet of covid that got updated daily and used it in my project to make a Flask Application. I tried various plots, also did some predictions for about next 5 days or so. This project introduced me to a lot problems in time series and how to tackle them, but also i learnt a lot on the side on web developments and wanted to explore more. Fun Fact, I got an opportunity to make a dashboard for the Government of Maharashtra for the same. But there, I predicted for the next 30 days for things like no. of cases, deaths, males, females and various other parameters. The MPE(Mean percentage error) of the prediction was 0.02 for # of Cases in India and that was very close.

Some Screenshots
You can check out the Opensource Dashboard that is a flask application and can predict for next 5 days. The algorithms used in GOVT. OF MH. dashboard are a bit different and we used complex algorithms like LSTMs, ARIMA etc. We cannot share it because of the NDA but its not much different
